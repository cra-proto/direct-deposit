Payments the CRA sends you

Payments the CRA sends you - Direct deposit

You may be looking for:

  • Make a payment to the CRA
 

If you have a Canadian bank or credit union account, you can use direct deposit to receive your tax refunds and benefit payments faster and securely. In the event of an emergency or unforeseen circumstance, using direct deposit will ensure you continue to get your payments on time.

Services and information

Individuals

Set up or update direct deposit online or with your Canadian bank or credit union

Businesses

Set up or update direct deposit online or with your Canadian bank or credit union

Trusts

Set up or update online or by mail with Form T3-DD, Direct Deposit Request for T3

Part XIII non-resident withholding tax

Use Form NR304, Direct Deposit Request for Non-Resident Account Holders and NR7-R Refund Applicants
